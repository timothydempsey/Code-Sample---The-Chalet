THE CHALET

Searching for another way to showcase your company? Participating at some of the world’s largest airshows doesn’t always mean exhibiting on the trade show floor. Dozens of companies have found budget-friendly alternative or supplemental solutions to bring their company to the international marketplace at The Chalet.

Chalets provide a unique, private upscale setting for meetings, presentations, high-end dining, product demonstrations, etc. adjacent to static display, the flight line and aerial demonstrations. The Chalet is a fully-staffed shared corporate hospitality venue holding privately-owned spaces and a shared dining room, located on-site alongside the private chalet units for many of the aerospace industry’s leading companies – providing highly visible cost-effective solutions that are specifically ideal for SMEs and companies with heavy meeting schedules.

The Chalet is staffed at all hours with an experienced professional team to assist you with any on-site requests, and to welcome your guests in the reception area with a smile. Guests check-in at the front desk and we’ll notify you of their arrival. A host of amenities and services including a business center and escorted personal transportation around the show are available at your disposal.

Kallman Worldwide, organizer of The Chalet, works with you to design a package for your company that is uniquely developed to suit your needs – this way you are paying for no more than what you need during the week. Contact us to begin building a customized package for your company at the next aerospace event. The Chalet is currently available at the Paris Air Show 2013, Dubai Airshow 2013 and Farnborough 2014.
